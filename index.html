Why Hangouts is a better alternative ?!

Unlike Google Hangouts, we store nothing about you. In fact we can't get anything about you, we use a technology called WebRTC that is in its esence a communication mean between multiple peers, all that we do is to make you exchange your information like the IP address, your codecs and some other stuff.. But in all, we're unable to store any of your videos, chats or anything else.
